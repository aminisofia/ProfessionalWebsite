(May - July 2024)

Table Top is a multiplayer online sandbox tabletop game constructed in Dreamlab.

I was hired to test Dreamlab's game engine while also participating in Discord's app pitches.

In this project:

✩ I learned web-networking and professional beta testing standards.

✩ Made assets in Adobe Illustrator.

✩ Designed UX/UI and gameplay.

✩ Collaborated in a team of three.

I designed this card deck based off of Discord's mascots, Clyde and Wumpus.

Using both procedural generation techniques and Illustrator to make the assets.
